Our Story

Why We Exist

We built Refractive after scaling a $40M eCommerce brand and seeing the gap firsthand: agencies with energy at the pitch, and silence after the contract.

Most agencies assign junior account managers who relay messages between you and the people doing the work. The people on your weekly call aren't the people building your campaigns, and nobody's looking at the full picture.

We operate differently. Refractive is an embedded growth team aligned to your business KPIs, not ad spend. Senior operators do the work directly. Every channel talks to every other channel. And we prove what's real with measurement that doesn't lie.
