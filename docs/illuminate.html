Illuminate FAQs

Yes. Illuminate™ is included with all Full-Stack Growth Partner retainers. You get attribution dashboards, incrementality testing, and weekly performance memos as part of the engagement.

Yes. Illuminate™ is available as a standalone product in Core and Pro tiers for brands managing media in-house or with another agency. You still get full access to dashboards, memos, and testing frameworks.

We design geo or cell-based split tests where one group sees your ads and another doesn't. By comparing the conversion rates between groups, we measure true incremental lift, not just what the ad platform claims credit for.

Attribution tracks individual user journeys. MMM looks at aggregate channel-level performance using statistical modeling. It's especially useful in privacy-restricted environments and for budget planning, since it doesn't depend on cookies or pixels.

Read-only access to your ad platforms, analytics, and Shopify store. We request the minimum permissions needed and never make changes to your live accounts without explicit approval.

Yes. All data is yours. Dashboards, reports, and raw exports are fully portable. If you ever leave, you take everything with you. No lock-in, no data hostage situations.

Attribution dashboards are live within 2 weeks of onboarding. Incrementality tests typically need 4-6 weeks to reach statistical significance. MMM models require 12+ months of historical data to calibrate.
