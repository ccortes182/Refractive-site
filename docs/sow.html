Proposal + SOW

Master Outline

This is the framework we use for every engagement. Modular by design—pick the pillars that match your growth stage, and we’ll scope the rest around your goals. Download the DOCX to customize per client.

1. Overview

Every engagement starts here. We define the playing field before we step on it.

  • Client name + scope summary — what we’re doing, at a glance
  • Objectives — the business outcomes we’re optimizing for (not vanity metrics)
  • Term + start date — when it begins, how long the initial engagement runs
  • Billing terms — payment schedule, invoicing cadence, late-payment terms

2. Scope of Work

Not every brand needs every module. We build the scope around your growth leaks—not a one-size-fits-all package. Select the modules that matter now; add more as you scale.

Module A: Foundation

Business strategy, brand positioning, GTM planning, creative strategy framework, and ops integrations. The strategic bedrock everything else builds on.

Module B: Growth

Paid social + search management, creative production pipeline, marketplace strategy, and analytics/reporting infrastructure. Where spend becomes signal.

Module C: Experience

Shopify development, CRO + A/B testing, SEO foundations, AI-powered enhancements, and Performance UX. Every click you pay for deserves a page that converts.

Module D: Retention

Email/SMS flows, loyalty + referral programs, subscription optimization, customer support systems, and affiliate management. The cheapest revenue you’ll earn.

Module E: Illuminate

Attribution dashboards, incrementality test plan, MMM planning, and a weekly budget memo. The measurement layer that keeps everything else honest.

3. Deliverables & Cadence

You’ll never wonder what’s happening. Our delivery rhythm is built for operators who want signal, not noise.

  • Weekly performance memo — what shipped, what’s next, where budget is moving. Drops every Monday.
  • Monthly roadmap — updated priorities, reallocations, and KPI check-in. The plan evolves with the data.
  • Quarterly strategy reset — big-picture review, upcoming bets, offer calendar, and budget scenario planning.

4. Responsibilities

Clear lines make fast teams. Here’s who owns what.

Refractive owns

Strategy development, campaign execution, creative production, test design + analysis, performance reporting, budget optimization, and measurement via Illuminate.

Client owns

Platform access (read-only initially), creative approvals within agreed SLAs, decision-making on strategic pivots, margin/goal inputs, and timely feedback on deliverables.

5. Measurement & KPIs

We define success before we start. No moving goalposts.

  • Baseline definition — where you are today, documented and agreed upon before any changes
  • KPI list — the 3–5 metrics that actually matter to your business, not a 40-row spreadsheet
  • Reporting frequency — weekly snapshots, monthly deep-dives, quarterly strategy reads

6. Performance Bonus (optional addendum)

When our incentives align with yours, everyone moves faster. This addendum puts skin in the game.

  • KPI definition — mutually agreed metrics (e.g., blended ROAS, contribution margin, LTV:CAC)
  • Baseline + thresholds — where we start and what “outperformance” means, in writing
  • Payment schedule — when and how the bonus is calculated and paid

7. Fees

Simple, transparent, aligned with outcomes.

  • Retainer or fixed project — flat monthly fee based on scope. No percent-of-spend. No hidden costs.
  • Out-of-scope rate card — if you need something beyond the SOW, you’ll know the rate before we start

8. Legal

Standard protections for both sides. Nothing exotic.

  • Confidentiality — mutual NDA covering all shared data and strategies
  • IP ownership — you own the work product. We own our methodologies and tools.
  • Data access — what we access, how we use it, and what happens when the engagement ends
  • Termination — 30-day written notice from either side. Clean exit, no drama.
